Hawiian Travels: Tourist spots

Pearl Harbor

This is some of the remaining wreckage from the famous attack on Pearl Harbor during World War II. It is located on the island of Oahu. While the actualmilitary base of Pearl Harbor is still active, these remains are open for the public to see.

Haleakala National Park

The Haleakala National Park is the site of the inactive volcano, specifiicaly the Haleakala Volcano. This location is full of breathtaking views and provides multiple avenues for tourists to take, including guided tourists and hiking.

Halawa Valley

Halaway Valley found in the Molokai island is one of if not the oldest settlement still standing today. This destination provides tours from some of the people still living in this settlement.

The Polynesian Cultural Center

Located on the island of Oahu, this location gives tourists a first hand view of the different cultures and daily lives of the various islands. The different cultures are sectioned into various areas at the center, providing a view of the different customs of each individual cultures.

These are only some of the fantastic locations you can find on the Hawiian Islands.
